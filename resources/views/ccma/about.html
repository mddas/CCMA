WELCOME TO CCMA

Accredited by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nepal (ICAN)

CCMA College is an accredited institution for CA professional studies by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nepal (ICAN). Since its foundation, the institution has been providing quality CA education through intensive coaching classes under both ICAN and ICAI in all levels along with required professional services.

The institution is thus a well recognized platform of toppers and CA alumni of the society. As of now CCMA has a pride of having more than 450 CAs produced and some of them have been already serving with us as the reputed faculty resources along with their practicing in CA Firms.

CCMA strives for instilling the required academic exposures along with the dynamic and ethical professionalism to the students.
